Assalamu Alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatuh

Welcome to Adur Muslim Centre

Adur Muslim Centre is the heart of the Muslim community in Shoreham-by-Sea and the wider Adur district. We serve brothers and sisters from Shoreham, Lancing, Southwick and surrounding areas, providing a warm and welcoming space for worship, education and community support.

Our doors are open to all five times a day for daily prayers, and we host Friday Jumu'ah, Eid prayers, Islamic education classes, and community events throughout the year. We welcome Muslims of all backgrounds and also warmly receive non-Muslim visitors who wish to learn about Islam.

We are committed to serving Allah (SWT) and our community with sincerity, brotherhood and compassion. May Allah bless our community and all who visit this centre. Ameen.

— The Imam and Management Committee,
Adur Muslim Centre

🕌

Our Mission

To provide a place of worship, learning and community for Muslims in the Adur district, following the teachings of the Quran and Sunnah of the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH).

🤝

Our Community

We serve a diverse Muslim community from various backgrounds — South Asian, Arab, African, and indigenous British Muslims — united by faith and love for Allah.

🌙

Open to All

We welcome non-Muslim visitors, interfaith dialogue, and those curious about Islam. Please contact us to arrange a visit.

Our History & Vision

◆
1995

Adur Muslim Centre Established

A small group of Muslim families in Shoreham came together to establish a place of worship for the growing Muslim community in the Adur district.

2000

Community Growth

The centre expanded its activities to include weekend Islamic education classes for children and regular lectures for adults.

2008

New Premises

The centre moved to its current location at 16 Brunswick Road, providing a larger space for the growing congregation.

2015

Ramadan & Eid Expansion

The centre began hosting large community iftars during Ramadan and Eid prayers for hundreds of worshippers from across West Sussex.

2020

Digital Outreach

During the pandemic, the centre launched online lectures and support services, connecting the community digitally.

2024

Ongoing Development

Plans for further development of facilities to better serve the needs of the growing Muslim community in the Adur district.

Our Services

◆
🕌

Daily Prayers

Five daily Salah in congregation. All welcome. Wudu facilities available.

📖

Jumu'ah

Friday congregational prayers at 1:15 PM and 1:45 PM. Khutbah in English and Arabic.

🌙

Ramadan

Tarawih prayers every night during Ramadan. Community iftar on weekends.

🎓

Islamic School

Weekend Quran and Islamic studies classes for children aged 5-16. Contact us to enrol.

👶

Nikah Ceremonies

Islamic marriage ceremonies conducted by the Imam. Contact us for more information.

🤲

Janazah

Funeral prayers and community support for bereaved families. Available when needed.

💬

Islamic Guidance

One-to-one Islamic counselling and guidance from the Imam. By appointment.

🏠

New Muslim Support

Support, mentoring and resources for those new to Islam. You are welcome here.

🤝

Community Events

Eid celebrations, charity drives, interfaith events and community gatherings throughout the year.
